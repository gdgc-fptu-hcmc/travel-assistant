AI-Powered Travel Assistant

A one-week sprint project by GDGoC FPT University Campus HCM

Project Overview

The AI-Powered Travel Assistant is an innovative project developed during a one-week sprint by the GDGoC team. This project aims to revolutionize travel planning by combining artificial intelligence with comprehensive travel data to provide personalized travel recommendations and assistance.
